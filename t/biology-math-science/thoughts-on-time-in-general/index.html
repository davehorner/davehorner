    Thoughts on time in general.

    Time is an interesting thing. Something we can graph and measure values against. It has a real perspective element to it. Everyone experiences their own sense of time. It is our attention span and our feelings of being alive. If we would be able to quantize our time as life, we could store values like our retinal patterns, taste, hearing, touch, and even emotions. I know that some of these things are not possible today, but given a sufficient lifespan, I believe computers will be able to make such quantization if things nano become true. (this of course is also a big if, but it is fun to think about anyways.)
