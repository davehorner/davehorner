    Drawing, painting, and other things

    learning to draw and illustrate takes time, patience, and practice...(hard things for many ppl)
    Draw, draw, draw - like 30 minutes a day.
    those who exercise their brain daily in artistic expression receive benefits outside the day's work being expressed.
    don't see your eye you see with your mind.
    no squealing, remember (that it's all in your head). useless not for long. the future is coming on.
    I believe, I believe every day's a good day when you paint. I believe it'll bring a lot of good thoughts to your mind.
