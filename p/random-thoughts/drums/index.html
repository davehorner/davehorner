    making your drums portable

    the electronic kit I got came with cables fit to length... and that wasn't leaving much room for change in arrangement. I am still trying to figure out the best way to setup my kit. I need longer wires; more piping; easier to turn knobs on the pvc joints, travel bag/case? I'd like to be able to treat my ekit as a kit that I can easily tear down transport and setup quickly. out of the box, my Yamaha DTX560 isn't as portable and easy to tear down and setup as I would have liked.
